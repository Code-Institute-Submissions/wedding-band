Packages Available

We offer various packages to suit your requirements. We can supply everything from ceremony music to pre-dinner reception to late night band and DJ. You can choose all, some or none of the extra add-ons listed below. You can create your own wedding entertainment package and only have to deal with one booking co-ordinator for everything. Please contact us to get a quote for your desired package.
